About this word counter

Content professionals juggle strict character limits for ads, newsletters, email subject lines and SERP snippets. Copy also needs to hit a comfortable reading time and maintain consistent structure. The Toolsivo Word & Character Counter keeps everything in one place: paste your copy, monitor stats, and preview how your title and description will look in Google.

Because the tool is client-side, you can safely paste drafts for regulated industries, confidential product launches or internal communication templates without risking leaks. Keep the tab open throughout your day—there’s no login, no tracking, and no storage of your text.

Common use cases

  • SEO writers: keep title tags under 60 characters and descriptions around 155.
  • Email marketers: ensure subject lines stay within recommended 35–45 characters for mobile.
  • Social media managers: plan threads or carousels by counting precise copy blocks.
  • Editors: track reading time to maintain consistent article depth.

How to interpret the metrics

Character counts include spaces, which aligns with most advertising and SEO guidelines. Words are calculated using whitespace separation, while line counts help you diagnose unwanted paragraphs. Reading time assumes ~200 words per minute and rounds to the nearest tenth for clarity.

FAQ

Does it support other languages?
Yes. Any language that uses whitespace-separated words will work. For languages without spaces, counts reflect characters.
Can I export the counts?
Copy/paste the stats manually or take a screenshot. This lightweight tool is intentionally simple and offline.
What about multi-paragraph formatting?
The line count helps you keep track of paragraphs. You can also copy the formatted text directly for CMS use.
